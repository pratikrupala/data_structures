Replace every element in the array with the greatest element on its right.

For the last element, there will be no element on its right. So replace it with -1.

Example,
Enter the size of the array: 5

Enter array elements: 
index[0]: 34
index[1]: 5
index[2]: 76
index[3]: 3
index[4]: 2

Array: 34 5 76 3 2 

Replaced array: 76 76 3 2 -1
